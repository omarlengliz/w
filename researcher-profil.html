About

Dr. James Wilson is a distinguished researcher in quantum computing with over 15 years of experience. His research focuses on quantum algorithms, quantum error correction, and applications of quantum computing in machine learning and cryptography. He leads the Quantum Algorithms Group at MIT's Quantum Institute and has published extensively in leading journals.
